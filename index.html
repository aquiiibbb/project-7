About the hotel

Book our affordable hotel in Searcy, AR

The Magnolia hotel offers an affordable stay in a prime location off I-57 at Exit 46. As our guest, you'll enjoy quick access to the shopping center next door, home to top coffee shops, restaurant chains and retail stores. Area attractions include Harding University, Arkansas State University, 18-hole golf courses, Searcy's Sports Complex, Berryhill Park and Unity Health Hospital—all less than a mile away. Or spend some time at the nearby playground, picnic areas and walking trails.

We're proud to offer enjoyable convenience and simplicity at our hotel. Stay connected with free WiFi throughout the property, start your morning with free coffee, and go for a swim in our seasonal outdoor pool. Business travelers appreciate our business center with fax and copy machines. And all travelers have access to free accessible parking, which includes truck and bus parking. Laundry facilities are also located on the property.

Each guest room comes equipped with a refrigerator, microwave, coffee maker, iron and ironing board, hair dryer and an HDTV with 160 channels (including premium movie channels). With a location just off the highway, the Econo Lodge Inn & Suites is easy to find, and our affordable rates are easy on your wallet.
